# Color Palette

Forge uses a dark command-center palette with controlled orange energy and neutral metallic surfaces.

## Core Palette

| Name | Hex | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Forge Orange | `#F6A86E` | Primary brand accent, active highlights, focused calls to action. |
| Ember Orange | `#D9752B` | Deeper accent, warning-adjacent warmth, forge glow, active borders. |
| Graphite | `#1E2229` | Primary surface, panels, dark cards, dashboard background. |
| Steel | `#2C313A` | Secondary surface, borders, sidebars, controls, tables. |
| Ash | `#C7CDD6` | Secondary text, icons, separators, inactive states. |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Highest contrast text and foreground elements. |
| Black | `#050608` | Deep background, hero surfaces, modal scrims. |

## Usage Ratios

| Color Group | Recommended Use |
| --- | --- |
| Black and Graphite | 60 to 70 percent of most dark surfaces. |
| Steel | 15 to 25 percent for panels, cards, borders, and table structure. |
| Ash and White | Text, icons, dividers, and high-contrast foreground elements. |
| Forge Orange and Ember Orange | 5 to 10 percent for active, selected, high-value, or brand-bearing elements. |

## Semantic Roles

| Semantic Role | Token | Hex |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Background base | Black | `#050608` |
| Background surface | Graphite | `#1E2229` |
| Background elevated | Steel | `#2C313A` |
| Text primary | White | `#FFFFFF` |
| Text secondary | Ash | `#C7CDD6` |
| Brand accent | Forge Orange | `#F6A86E` |
| Brand accent strong | Ember Orange | `#D9752B` |

## Accessibility

- Do not use orange as the only indicator of state.
- Pair status colors with labels and icons.
- Use White on Graphite or Black for high-contrast primary text.
- Use Ash for secondary text only on dark backgrounds.
- Test orange text carefully; prefer orange for borders, icons, glows, and small accents rather than long body copy.

## Avoid

- One-note orange interfaces.
- Neon science-fiction palettes.
- Bright consumer gradients.
- Beige, tan, or brown-dominant themes.
- Blue enterprise dashboards that erase the Forge identity.
