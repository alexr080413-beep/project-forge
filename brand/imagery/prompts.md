# Imagery Prompts

Forge imagery should support a dark, operational, defense-technology mood without drifting into spectacle.

## Imagery Style

Use:

- Dark command centers
- Tactical maps
- Mountain terrain
- Planning cells
- Intelligence workspaces
- Orange interface overlays
- Semi-realistic defense technology aesthetic

Avoid:

- Explosions
- Sci-fi armor
- Generic business stock photos
- Excessive flags
- Weapons-focused imagery
- Fantasy war rooms
- Overly cinematic fire and smoke
- Unreadable hologram clutter

## Prompt Template

```text
Create a dark command-center visual for Forge, an open-source digital operations platform for Exercise Control.
Scene: <specific operational scene>.
Mood: precise, disciplined, military-enterprise, calm under pressure.
Visual language: graphite and black environment, steel surfaces, restrained orange interface overlays, realistic screens, readable operational posture.
Avoid: explosions, sci-fi armor, weapons focus, excessive flags, generic business stock photography, cartoon style, fantasy elements.
No text unless explicitly requested.
```

## Example Prompts

### Command Center

```text
Create a semi-realistic dark command center for Exercise Control, with controllers monitoring an operational timeline, review queues, and tactical map displays. Use graphite surfaces, steel equipment, restrained orange interface overlays, and a calm military-enterprise mood. Avoid explosions, weapons focus, excessive flags, and generic business stock photography. No text.
```

### Tactical Map

```text
Create a close-up of a tactical planning map on a dark operations desk, with subtle orange digital overlays, event markers, timeline references, and scenario planning notes. Style should be precise, realistic, and defense-technology inspired. Avoid weapons, flags, explosions, and sci-fi hologram clutter. No text.
```

### Mountain Terrain

```text
Create a moody mountain training terrain scene at dawn with subtle command-center data overlays in Forge Orange, suggesting exercise planning and terrain awareness. Keep the scene realistic, restrained, and operational. Avoid combat imagery, explosions, weapons, and fantasy elements. No text.
```

### Intelligence Workspace

```text
Create a dark intelligence controller workspace with multiple displays showing source review, entity lookup, scenario translation, and product review queues. Use graphite, steel, ash, and restrained orange accents. The mood should be precise, focused, and audit-ready. Avoid generic office stock style, sci-fi armor, weapons, and excessive flags. No text.
```

### Planning Cell

```text
Create a planning cell environment for a military exercise control team, with a table, laptops, maps, notes, and large wall displays. Use a dark command-center palette with subtle orange highlights. The scene should feel professional, joint, and coalition-ready without showing specific flags or weapons. No text.
```

## Composition Rules

- Leave negative space for headlines in website or presentation images.
- Keep screens plausible and restrained.
- Use orange overlays as an accent, not the whole image.
- Prefer human operators as silhouettes or background participants unless the image needs a specific role.
- Keep visual focus on operations, planning, intelligence, review, and assessment.
