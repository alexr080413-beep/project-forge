# Arctic Operations

## Scenario

A notional arctic operations exercise focused on cold-weather movement, communications degradation, partner coordination, and sustainment under extreme conditions.

## Training Audience

Joint cold-weather task force staff and supporting logistics, intelligence, communications, and Exercise Control cells.

## Objectives

- Plan and execute movement in extreme cold.
- Maintain communications and reporting discipline.
- Manage sustainment and casualty evacuation under weather constraints.

## Expected Products

- Arctic weather advisory.
- Movement status update.
- Communications degradation report.
- Sustainment risk brief.
- AAR finding on cold-weather decision-making.

## Example Injects

- Whiteout conditions.
- Vehicle cold-start failure.
- Satellite communications degradation.
- Medical evacuation delay.
- Partner force coordination request.
