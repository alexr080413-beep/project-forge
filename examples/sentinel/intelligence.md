# Sentinel Intelligence

## Baseline Threat Picture

The opposing force is a fictional near-peer aligned proxy network operating in mountain terrain.

Capabilities include:

- Small team reconnaissance.
- Electronic warfare support.
- Small unmanned aircraft.
- Local proxy influence.
- Limited cyber disruption.
- Information environment amplification.
