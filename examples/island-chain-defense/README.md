# Island Chain Defense

## Scenario

A notional maritime and littoral defense exercise involving distributed units, contested logistics, weather disruption, and multi-domain coordination.

## Training Audience

Marine littoral regiment staff, naval coordination cell, logistics planners, and Exercise Control.

## Objectives

- Maintain distributed command and control.
- Coordinate logistics across island nodes.
- Respond to surveillance, weather, and mobility constraints.

## Expected Products

- Maritime situation report.
- Logistics risk summary.
- Weather impact update.
- Commander decision brief.
- AAR finding on distributed control.

## Example Injects

- Port access delay.
- Unmanned aircraft sighting.
- Fuel resupply interruption.
- Tropical storm track change.
- Communications outage.
