# Humanitarian Assistance

## Scenario

A notional humanitarian assistance exercise after severe coastal storm damage. Exercise Control coordinates civil authorities, logistics, medical support, and public information challenges.

## Training Audience

Joint task force staff and supporting civil-military coordination cells.

## Objectives

- Coordinate humanitarian logistics under degraded infrastructure.
- Manage information flow between military and civil partners.
- Prioritize medical evacuation, shelter, and supply distribution decisions.

## Expected Products

- Situation update.
- Logistics status report.
- Public information release.
- Medical support request.
- AAR coordination finding.

## Example Injects

- Bridge closure.
- Hospital generator failure.
- Displaced population surge.
- Media question about aid delays.
- Conflicting shelter capacity report.
