# Product Vision

Forge becomes the digital operating system for military training exercises.

Every Event.

Every Inject.

Every Exercise.

## Product North Star

Forge should reduce administrative friction so Exercise Control teams can spend more time planning, executing, and improving military training.

## Long-Term Direction

Forge should grow into an exercise-centered platform that supports:

- Planning.
- Preparation.
- Execution.
- Assessment.
- Archive.
- Product generation and review.
- Operational Knowledge Graph.
- Mission Replay.
- Operational analytics.
- Plugin and profile ecosystems.
- Forge Academy training.

## Reference Exercise

Project Sentinel is the reference implementation used to validate product direction and demonstrate Forge capabilities.
