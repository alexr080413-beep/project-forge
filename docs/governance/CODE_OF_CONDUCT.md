# Code Of Conduct

Forge contributors are expected to maintain a professional, mission-focused, and respectful environment.

## Standards

Contributors should:

- Use respectful language.
- Assume good intent while reviewing rigorously.
- Focus criticism on the work, not the person.
- Preserve operational safety and human authority.
- Avoid sharing sensitive, private, classified, or real exercise data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, doxxing, discriminatory language, intentional disruption, or pressure to bypass review, security, or documentation standards.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to project maintainers through the appropriate private channel for the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, moderate, or restrict participation to protect the project and contributors.
