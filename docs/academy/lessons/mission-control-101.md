# Mission Control 101

Mission Control is the operational picture for the selected exercise.

## What You See

- Active exercise.
- Exercise status and phase.
- Controller status.
- Pending reviews.
- Open injects.
- Products generated.
- Timeline summary.
- Latest activity.

## What It Is For

Use Mission Control to answer: "What is happening in the exercise right now, and what needs human attention?"

## Human Control Point

Mission Control may show pending work, but it does not bypass review authority.
