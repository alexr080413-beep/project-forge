# Intelligence Chief Learning Path

The Intelligence Chief uses Forge to maintain situational awareness, review intelligence updates, and connect operational assets to exercise objectives.

## Recommended Sequence

1. [Mission Control 101](../lessons/mission-control-101.md)
2. [Atlas 101](../lessons/atlas-101.md)
3. [Knowledge Graph 101](../lessons/knowledge-graph-101.md)
4. [Exercise Library 101](../lessons/exercise-library-101.md)
5. [Build Exercise In Atlas](../task-guides/build-exercise-in-atlas.md)
6. [Validate Exercise](../task-guides/validate-exercise.md)
7. [Complete Exercise Walkthrough](../walkthroughs/complete-exercise-walkthrough.md)

## Intelligence Standard

Every intelligence update should preserve source context, scenario consistency, and traceability to objectives or operational assets.
